JAM PACK APP DESIGN

Busy travelers seek to minimize time and money spent on fees and checking bags while maximizing trip enjoyment. Existing packing list generators are too generic. Jam Pack provides personalized packing suggestions via a questionnaire based on the traveler's personality and travel habits. Users can also input their bags and view sorted lists accordingly.

ROLE

User Research, App Design

TIMELINE

3 Weeks

PROCESS

UX Research, Information Architecture, Prototyping, User Testing, Visual Design

THE USER

Based on interviews with millennial travelers, I found some common issues in the process of packing and checking bags. My typical frequent traveler has little personal time and needs a regular packing system oriented for their individual needs so they can spend their hard-earned time and money on trips instead of lines and fees.

THE PROBLEM

Users resent having to pay for luggage on flights, especially for carry-on bags. Checking in bags costs money and time that most travelers do not have to spend.

Problem Statement

Rachel, a hardworking millennial on a budget, needs a way to pack more effectively and economically because she wastes a lot of time and money checking bags.
